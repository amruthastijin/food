BEEF FRY

Kerala beef fry is a dish made of beef, slow-roasted in a mixture of spices, onions, curry leaves, and coconut slivers, fried in coconut oil. The dish is also popularly known in Kerala as "Beef Ullarthiyathu"



MASALA DOSA

Masala Dosa / dosey / dosai is a variation of the popular South Indian Cuisine dosa which has its origins in Udupi cuisine of Karnataka. It is made from rice, lentils, potato, fenugreek, ghee and curry leaves, and served with chutneys and sambar. It is popular in South India
